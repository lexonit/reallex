# Landing Page Updates - Enterprise CRM Offering

## Overview
The landing page has been completely updated to reflect a professional, enterprise-grade CRM solution with full customization capabilities, in-house deployment options, and 6 months of premium support.

## Key Changes Made

### 1. **Hero Section**
- **New Headline**: "Enterprise CRM Built for [Custom Solutions, In-House Control, Enterprise Ready, Complete Support]"
- **Updated Tagline**: Emphasizes custom deployment on your infrastructure with 6 months support
- **Call-to-Action**: Changed from "14-Day Free Trial" to "Get Custom Quote" and "View Features"
- **Badge**: Updated from "v2.0 AI Pilot Update" to "Enterprise-Grade CRM: Production Ready & Fully Customizable"

### 2. **Features Section**
Replaced AI-focused features with enterprise capabilities:

| Old Feature | New Feature |
|---|---|
| AI Lead Scoring | Fully Customizable Platform |
| Predictive Analytics | Production Ready Deployment |
| Instant Automation | 6 Months Premium Support |

### 3. **Product Showcase**
Updated the three-step product showcase:

| Step | Old Title | New Title |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Your Daily Command Center | Complete CRM Dashboard |
| 2 | A Pipeline That Actually Moves | Powerful Pipeline Management |
| 3 | The White-Glove Client Portal | Secure Client Portal |

Each with updated descriptions emphasizing customization and enterprise readiness.

Complete restructuring for custom deployment model:

**Old Pricing Model:**
